Upcoming Classes

Explore our upcoming classes designed to provide hands-on learning and practical knowledge in various subjects. Each class is structured to ensure a deep understanding of the topic and foster skill development.

Introduction to Programming

Duration: 4 hours

This class will guide students through writing their first Python programs. The structure includes:

  • 1 hour of introductory material
  • Writing three programs:
    • A "Hello World" program
    • An API call to display information from the web
    • A simple game

No prior experience is required.

VR Development

Duration: 4 hours

This class introduces the basics of using Unity for VR development on Oculus Headsets. Students will learn to:

  • Interface their computer with the provided headsets
  • Develop a VR scene using object recognition on images and models powered by artificial intelligence

ACT Prep

Duration: 4 sessions, each 4 hours

We will cover one major section of the ACT each week. This comprehensive course includes:

  • Advanced test-taking strategies, tips, and tricks
  • Foundational knowledge underlying the test

With a focus on strategy, students can expect to improve their scores significantly. By combining strategic techniques with foundational knowledge, your child will excel on the ACT.

Basics of AI and Machine Learning

Duration: 4 hours

This class introduces the foundations of machine learning. The session includes:

  • 2 hours of foundational concepts
  • A hands-on project where students implement their own computer vision project
